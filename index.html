About Us

Bryan's Cafe is a family-owned cafe that has been serving the Sydney community since 2005. We are passionate about providing fresh, high-quality food and beverages in a warm and welcoming environment. Our menu is inspired by seasonal produce and classic cafe favourites.

Our History

Bryan's Cafe was founded by Bryan Mitchell in 2005 with a simple goal: to create a place where people could enjoy great food and feel at home. Starting with a single location in Surry Hills, Sydney, Bryan's Cafe has grown to three locations across Sydney NSW. Our commitment to quality and community remains at the heart of everything we do.
