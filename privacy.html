Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Bryan's Cafe is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and store your information in accordance with the Privacy Act 1988 (Australia).

2. What Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you provide through our enquiry form, including your first name, last name, email address, and message. We only collect information that is necessary to respond to your enquiry.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your personal information is used only to respond to your enquiry. We do not sell, share, or distribute your information to any third parties. Your information is stored securely and is only accessible by authorised staff.

4. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to the personal information we hold about you, and to request that any inaccurate information be corrected. You may also request that your information be deleted from our records.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: info@bryanscafe.com.au
